How to Play

  1. Study the visual or text hints provided to deduce the hidden number.
  2. Input your guess and receive feedback on how close your guess is.
  3. Continue guessing until you uncover the mystery number.

Gameplay Modes

  • Normal Mode: Guess the hidden number using visual hints.
  • Blind Play: Challenge yourself with text-based hints only.
  • Daily Challenge: Compete against the clock with new puzzles daily.
